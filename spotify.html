Spotify Concept

Spotify is the most popular global audio streaming service with 365m users, including 165m subscribers across 178 markets.

Role: Product Designer

This is an unshipped Passion Project in which I wanted to explore what other versions of Spotify could exist. I wanted to prioritize the user's listening experience above all else and created three different designs, all from scratch (no templates!)

Conservative Design

As designers, we often have to work within a design system that already exists. This version of my design cleans up the UI and makes the information more presentable to the user, while still keeping familiar elements.

Playful Design

This version focuses on the music currently playing and takes greater design liberties while still maintaining the overall look and feel. This top section can be expanded with lyrics or other information about the song.

Extreme Design

This version focuses two things many music lovers obsess over: album art and the music itself (of course). This design had the user's sole immersive experience in mind.
