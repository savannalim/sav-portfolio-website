Redesigning Auburn Green:

Auburn Green is a big, triangular-shaped plot of land in the middle of Ithaca that was in the middle of a commercial and residential hub. We believe it is being underused and undervalued, and that it can bring much more value to the neighborhood it lives in.

Role: Urban Designer

Experiences: User Research, User Interviews, Hand Drawing, Movement Flow Analysis, Landscape Architecture

Approach:

I wanted to study several things in the park: movement, traffic, bodies, businesses around the park, and topography. In the process images below, you'll see that I studied and theorized the movement of peoples' bodies through the park with different design approaches. I also interviewed local businesses and townspeople about the park's purpose, how often they use the park, how safe they felt in the park, and what would make them visit the park more. I also wanted to know more about their problems and their biggest deterrent from entering the park.
